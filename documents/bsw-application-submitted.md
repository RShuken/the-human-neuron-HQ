**Session Title:** The Human Neuron: Become Part of a Live Neural Network

**Session Description:**
What if you could feel what it's like to be inside a neural network — not by watching a diagram, but by becoming one?

The Human Neuron is a first-of-its-kind interactive experience where every person in the room becomes a single neuron in a live, functioning neural network. Using just your phone (scan a QR code — no app download required), you'll join a real-time collective system projected on screen, where your individual choices ripple through the network to produce emergent intelligence.

Through a series of guided rounds, you'll experience firsthand how signals propagate through layers of neurons, how activation thresholds determine which pathways fire, how the network learns which pathways work and which don't, and how repeated patterns strengthen connections — just like your brain and just like the AI systems shaping our world.

This isn't a lecture about AI. It's an embodied experience that makes abstract concepts visceral and intuitive. You won't just learn how neural networks produce the next token — you'll feel it happen as your neuron fires (or doesn't) based on the weighted signals from the people around you.

Whether you're a founder trying to understand the AI landscape, an engineer curious about the mechanics under the hood, or someone who's heard "neural network" a thousand times but never quite clicked with the concept — this session will give you an understanding you can't get from slides.

Built by a team combining deep technical AI expertise, knowledge graph visualization, and experience design, The Human Neuron bridges the gap between AI theory and human intuition. You'll leave with a gut-level understanding of how these systems learn, adapt, and create — because you will have been part of one.

Bring your phone. Become a neuron. Experience AI from the inside.
